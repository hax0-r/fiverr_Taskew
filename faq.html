    We support several compliance frameworks including SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA, PCI DSS 4.0.1, NIST CSF 2.0, and GDPR with the EU-US Data Privacy Framework.

  • What packages does Taskew offer for compliance and security programs?

    We offer two packages:

    Advanced: For teams scaling and operating compliance and security programs, includes everything in Standard plus risk assessment reports, multiple approvers for policies, custom fields on controls, custom risk scoring dimensions, and custom fields on risk scenarios.
